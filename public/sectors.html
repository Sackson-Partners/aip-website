Energy & Mining
Powering industrialisation and lighting up the continent. From utility-scale solar to off-grid solutions, AIP structures bankable energy deals across the full generation and distribution value chain. Beyond power, AIP actively covers Mining & Critical Minerals — including gold, lithium, cobalt, manganese, and rare earth extraction projects — connecting mining sponsors with project finance, streaming arrangements, and royalty capital. Africa holds over 30% of the world's critical mineral reserves, and AIP is uniquely positioned to structure investment-grade deals across the full mining value chain: exploration, extraction, processing, and logistics.
