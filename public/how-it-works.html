5-Step Pipeline

Every project follows
the same rigorous path

Step 01
Project Intake
Sponsors submit infrastructure projects with financial models, technical docs, and ESG data through the platform.
Step 02
Verification
PETFEL AI engine runs automated due diligence. Country-based Focal Points validate on the ground.
Step 03
Data Rooms
Qualified investors access secure, structured document rooms for deeper review of bankable assets.
Step 04
Due Diligence
Full EIN report generated. PETFEL score issued. Red-flags cleared before investor introduction.
Step 05
Deal Rooms
Capital matched to project. Term sheets negotiated. AIP supports through to financial close.
