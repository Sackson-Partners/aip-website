Who We Are

Beyond a marketplace — a complete ecosystem for delivery

We are an independent investment and advisory platform dedicated to closing Africa's infrastructure delivery gap. We support the development, verification, and financing of high-quality infrastructure projects across the continent, going beyond a marketplace to build a complete ecosystem for delivery.

By leveraging advanced technology, data analytics, and deep industry expertise, we remove the traditional frictions associated with investing in African infrastructure — equipping project owners with the tools to become bankable, while providing global investors with a transparent, de-risked pipeline of high-impact opportunities.

Leadership

Our Founders

Vamo Soko Sako
Founder & Chief Executive Officer
INSEAD · Sorbonne

Vamo brings over two decades of experience in African infrastructure finance and project structuring. He has advised governments and private sponsors across the energy, infrastructure, Mining, and digital sectors, closing deals across sub-Saharan Africa. His vision is to make Africa's infrastructure investment ecosystem as transparent and accessible as any global market.

Dr. Bintu Zahara Sakor
Co-Founder & Head of Research
Harvard · University of Oslo · PRIO

Dr. Sakor leads AIP's research and verification frameworks, drawing on her academic work in conflict-sensitive development and political economy across fragile states. Her proprietary PETFEL methodology combines AI-driven risk assessment with deep field knowledge, setting the standard for due diligence in African infrastructure investment.
