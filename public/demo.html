Phase 3 · PETFEL Engine
PETFEL Due Diligence
Six-dimension institutional-grade assessment

AIP's proprietary PETFEL framework scores projects across six critical dimensions — Political, Economic, Technical, Financial, Environmental, and Legal. Each dimension is verified by both AI workflows and a human analyst overlay, producing an audit-ready due diligence report used by DFIs, pension funds, and sovereign wealth funds.

P — Political: governance stability, regulatory framework, government support
E — Economic: macro-fiscal context, affordability, market demand
T — Technical: feasibility, engineering readiness, procurement status
F — Financial: model bankability, IRR, offtake risk, blended finance potential
E — Environmental: ESG compliance, climate risk, community impact
L — Legal: title clarity, concession validity, permit completeness
